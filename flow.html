Flow of Event

1

Day 1 – Idea Pitch

Event starts at 10:30 AM. Teams will be given specific time slots for presentations.
Each team presents their idea via PPT (format given at registration).
7 minutes presentation + 3–5 minutes Q&A with judges.
Evaluation based on clarity, originality, and feasibility.
Top teams get shortlisted for Round 2.
2

Day 2 – Prototype + Demo

Event starts at 10:30 AM. Teams will be allocated specific time slots for demos.
Shortlisted teams submit GitHub repo + 3–5 min demo video.
Each team gets 12–15 minutes (5 min demo + rest Q&A).
Evaluation based on working prototype, technical depth, innovation, usability, and impact.
Winners announced at the end of Day 2.
